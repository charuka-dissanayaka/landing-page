We’re Redefining The Primary Education Experience For International Families In Singapore.

School fees for international students in Singapore are infamously expensive. Every parent wants to give their child excellent, quality education, but unfortunately, this is out of reach for many international families living in Singapore.

The limited number of places in national schools in Singapore for international students effectively takes away the ability for parents to choose a school for their child on a cost-benefit basis on a fee spectrum.

The Open Primary was founded to give parents the power of choice again.

We connect students from P1-P6 with qualified, NIE trained and MOE experienced teachers and close-to-home class centres, creating small class sizes (approximately 9 students) for students to learn conductively and effectively. Our teachers will guide students through the local Singapore primary school curriculum for a full academic year with regular assessments and reports each term, but without the high stress of external exams (read “PSLE”).

Your child deserves the best, but that doesn’t have to come at exorbitant prices. Find out more about our one-of-a-kind programme today.

About The open Primary

The Open Primary was founded in 2016 to offer an alternative path to that of national and international schools in Singapore, offering a unique, hybridised blend of affordable education in a private setting for international students to receive the best education Singapore has to offer.

We believe that all families should have access to high quality education at a cost they consider reasonable for the expected educational outcomes, and that’s exactly what we seek to do.
